# Shmyg Seed Prompt

Purpose: approved seed frame for the one-time onboarding guide character.

Source fiction: `docs/setting.md`, especially "Voice Guide: Shmyg".

## Visual Spec

- Small soot-stained goblin potion-brewer and workshop tinkerer.
- Respectful, nervous expression; competent rather than comic relief.
- Workshop clothing: patched leather apron, brass/copper fittings, tiny tools, singed cuffs.
- Readable face at small mobile HUD scale.
- Chunky dark industrial fantasy game-sprite tone, matching the current goblin fortress art direction.
- Three-quarter front view, full body, centered, generous padding.
- Transparent runtime output; generate on flat chroma-key background first for local alpha cleanup.

Use case: stylized-concept

Primary request: create one full-body seed frame of Shmyg, a small soot-stained goblin potion-brewer and workshop tinkerer who acts as a nervous respectful guide for a young king.
Scene/backdrop: perfectly flat solid #00ff00 chroma-key background for background removal.

Style/medium: polished bitmap game sprite, hand-painted 2D, chunky dark industrial fantasy, production sprite tone, not photorealistic, not vector.
Composition/framing: isolated full-body character, three-quarter front view, centered, generous padding, readable silhouette, no crop.
Lighting/mood: soft sprite lighting with warm brass and magma-orange accents, cautious but helpful mood.
Color palette: dark iron, soot black, muted goblin green, worn brass/copper, small warm orange accents.
Constraints: background must be one uniform #00ff00 with no shadows, gradients, texture, floor plane, lighting variation, or reflection; do not use #00ff00 inside the character; no text, no labels, no watermark, no UI frame.
